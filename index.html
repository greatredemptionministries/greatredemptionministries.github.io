About Us

The above quotation from John 8:36, was the foundational scripture for the establishment of Great Redemption Ministries Inc. in 2006. The name "Great Redemption Ministries" was brought about as an inspiration from a Pastor when Mr. Andrews Frimpong received the calling to establish the church. We affirm the Holy Bible as the inspired Word of God and the basis for our belief. We voluntarily band ourselves together as a body of baptized believers in Jesus Christ, personally committed to sharing the good news of salvation to lost mankind.
